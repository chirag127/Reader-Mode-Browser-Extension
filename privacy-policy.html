Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 8, 2023

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Reader Mode Browser Extension ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our browser extension. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected and that you understand how your data is handled.

By using the Reader Mode Browser Extension, you agree to the data practices described in this policy.

Required Permissions

Our extension requires certain permissions to function properly. Here's why each permission is necessary:

  • activeTab: Allows the extension to access the currently active tab. This is necessary to extract and process the content of the webpage you're viewing for reader mode.
  • scripting: Enables the extension to execute scripts on the active tab to extract content and apply reader mode transformations.
  • storage: Allows the extension to store your preferences (like font size, theme choice) and temporarily store extracted content between page loads.
  • contextMenus: Enables the right-click menu option for reading selected text aloud.
  • host permissions (<all_urls>): Allows the extension to work on any website you visit. This is necessary for the reader mode functionality to be available across the web.

Information We Collect

The Reader Mode Browser Extension collects and processes the following information:

  • User Preferences: We store your preferences such as font size, font family, and theme choice (light/dark mode) to provide a consistent experience.
  • Temporary Content Data: When you activate reader mode, we temporarily extract and process the content of the webpage you're viewing. This data is stored in your browser's local storage and is not transmitted to any external servers.

What We DO NOT Collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Browsing history
  • Login credentials or passwords
  • Any data from secure (https) form fields
  • Any data from private/incognito browsing sessions

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to provide and improve the Reader Mode functionality:

  • User preferences are used to customize your reading experience according to your choices.
  • Temporary content data is used to transform the webpage into reader mode format.
  • For the text-to-speech feature, we process the text content to be read aloud using your browser's built-in Web Speech API.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. All processing happens locally within your browser.

Data Storage & Security

All data collected by the Reader Mode Browser Extension is stored locally on your device using the browser's built-in storage mechanisms:

  • User preferences are stored in the browser's local storage.
  • Temporary content data is stored in the browser's session storage and is cleared when you close the tab or navigate away.

No data is transmitted to external servers, and all processing happens locally within your browser. This means your data remains on your device and is subject to your browser's security measures.

Third-Party Services

The Reader Mode Browser Extension uses the following third-party services:

  • Mozilla's Readability.js: We use this open-source library to extract and clean article content. This processing happens entirely within your browser.
  • Web Speech API: For the text-to-speech feature, we use your browser's built-in Web Speech API. The processing of speech synthesis happens locally on your device.

These services do not receive any data from our extension; all processing happens locally within your browser.

User Rights

As a user of the Reader Mode Browser Extension, you have the right to:

  • Access: View the data stored by the extension in your browser's local storage.
  • Modify: Change your preferences at any time through the extension's interface.
  • Delete: Clear all stored data by resetting the extension or uninstalling it from your browser.

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
